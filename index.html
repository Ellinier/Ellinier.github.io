Research Highlights

My research topic lies at the intersection of Robotics, terramechanics, and computer vision, specifically, focusing on autonomous, agile navigation, and scientific exploration for space robots. Her current research topic is integrating environmental physical property perception into robot’s locomotion and navigation to handle slippery and soft deformable terrains.

Physical Environment Representation and Perception
